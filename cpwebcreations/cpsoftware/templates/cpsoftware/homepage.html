Technologies

Technology

At CP Software Creation, we use modern and reliable tools to deliver solutions that combine functionality, security, and flexibility.

Python: A clean and versatile programming language, suitable for all types of applications.

Django: A powerful Python web framework, ideal for building secure and scalable websites.

HTML: The foundation of every website – used to structure and organise your content.

CSS: We customise the look and feel of your site to match your style and requirements.

JavaScript: Adds interactivity and dynamic behaviour wherever needed.

SQL / PostgreSQL: Reliable solutions for storing and managing data quickly and consistently.

Heroku: A cloud platform for hosting and managing live applications with ease.

Kivy: A Python framework for developing mobile apps across multiple platforms.

Tkinter: Ideal for desktop apps with multiple windows and a simple interface.

We choose the right tools for each project based on your needs and goals, to ensure the best possible outcome.
